The Story
You, Detective Cabbage, are selected to investigate one of biggest chemical conflicts ever! Recently, Apple CEO Mr. Tim Cook was cooking his famous Stuffed Pepper Soup. Suddenly, a mysterious figure ran into her kitchen and ruined the soup with an unkown substance. After much investigation, the forensics team has narrowed the crime to two suspects: Acid Archie and Basic Ben. It’s your job to find out who is behind this mischief and bring justice to the Mr. Cook.
